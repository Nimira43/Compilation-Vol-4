The Ashes of Khazer

Peace, whilst fragile, has held for over twenty years. But trouble is stirring in the outer rims of the Republic.

On the outpost world of Khazer defiant imperialists, led by the sinister ADMIRAL MARR, are ransacking the planet. There are rumours they are seeking some kind of ancient relic.

Meanwhile, REY SKYWALKER, overseeing the early stages of the SKYWALKER ORDER, has felt strange disturbances in the Force, disturbances of immense power....
