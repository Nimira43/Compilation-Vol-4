Episode XI

The Hunt for the Wraith

Victory over the forces of ADMIRAL MARR are short-lived. In neighbouring systems whole communities are suffering from a strange PESTILENCE

Panic is rift and disorder threatens to spread throughout the Republic. The SKYWALKER ORDER are sent to investigate what people are calling THE WRAITH.

REY SKYWALKER, troubled by unanswered questions, leads the investigation, trying to unravel the motives of MARR and the resulting pandemic....
